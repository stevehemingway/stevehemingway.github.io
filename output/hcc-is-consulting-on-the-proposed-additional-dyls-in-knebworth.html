HCC is consulting on the proposed additional DYLs in Knebworth.

Published: Tue 17 April 2018
Updated: Sat 09 May 2020
By steve

In Knebworth.

tags: NHDC politics

Herts County Council is consulting on the proposed additional double yellow lines in Gun Lane and Park Lane. Objections to the proposals should be made in writing to Terry Curtis, Postal Point (CH0242) First Floor, East Link Corridor, County Hall, Pegs Lane, Hertford SG13 8DN or tro.team@hertfordshire.gov.uk by 26 April 2018.

You can comment via the website at https://www.hertfordshire.gov.uk/services/highways-roads-and-pavements/changes-to-your-road/traffic-regulation-orders/traffic-regulation-orders.aspx (search for “NH00618” on the page).
