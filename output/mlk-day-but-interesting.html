MLK day, but interesting

Published: Mon 17 January 2022
Updated: Tue 16 August 2022
By steve

In Markets.

Monday 17, January 2022

Wrap

Interest rates are on the move! Eurodollar futures continue to trade lower. Bonds, US and EU are weaker. Commodities are generally up: oil has had a very strong run since before Christmas. This almost exactly matches the systematic move up in 10Y yields for Treasuries. This key rate is now 1.79%. Usually, such a move would weaken the USD vs JPY, but the movement has not been consistent. The Yen has weakened from around 113 to 114, but the move has not been monotonic.
