Aluminium glitters brighter than gold

Published: Sun 15 August 2021
Updated: Wed 01 December 2021
By steve

In markets.

Weekend 14/15 August

Goldmans say that Aluminium is going skywards. EVs need a lot of the stuff, 250 kg per car. It takes a lot of energy to make, and that’s going up too. How to play this: futures or maybe Rusal, if you aren’t afraid of Moscow stocks.
